Iuvō: Peer-to-Peer Lending Service

Branding / Animation / Website UI

Iuvō is a safe, community-based platform rooted in the trust of its users that helps members secure small, short-term loans. The name Iuvō comes from the latin word to support or to aid. Its mission is to create a mutually beneficial space for young Canadians to build financial health during the ongoing housing affordability crisis.
