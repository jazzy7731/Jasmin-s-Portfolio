  • Hiya!

    I'm Jasmin

    (without the e)

    I am a "jazzy" branding and communications designer based out of Vancouver, BC. I grew up in Whistler surrounded by the mountains and forests inspiring my creativity.

    I began my studies at Vancouver Film School earning a diploma in digital design before transferring into the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University to complete my Bachelor of Design in Visual Communication.

    I am a multidisciplinary storyteller focused in art direction, motion design and illustration. I like to think my many odd jobs— including balloon bouquets, knitting lessons and tourism—have helped me improve my skills and be ready to solve any problem thrown my way!

    When I'm not in front of my computer, fiddling with layers and keyframes, I have my nose in a book or you can find me adding scraps of paper to one of my many creative journals.
