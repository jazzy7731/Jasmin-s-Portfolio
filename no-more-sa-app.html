No More App

Branding / UI / Collaboration

Sexual assault survivors often experience isolation. No More is a community-based app that connects them to other survivors so they heal together. The project was a collaborative effort with branding designer Angie Pano and UX/UI designer Jill Tao. My roles were project management, brand development and collateral design.
