Unwind: Quiet Cafe

Branding / Packaging / Illustration

Unwind is a community coffee shop, or “stress relief cafe.” The goal is to create a safe, calming cafe environment—just like your living room—perfect for not just a calming, sound-conscious experience, but also for social activities like book clubs and knitting groups.
