Punk's Not Dead it just went POP!

Poster Design / Animation / Collateral

Punk’s not Dead it just went POP! Is a music festival celebrating the revival of punk music with a twist of 21st century pop. The project showcases pop punk artists in a collage style heavily inspired by punk posters from the 80s.
